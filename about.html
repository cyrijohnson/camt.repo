Comfoter Action Mission Trust

Comforer Action Mission Trust (CAMT) was conceived and eastablished in the late 1990's by the joint efforts of friends, many kind hearts and wellwishers as a non-profit, non-goverment orgaization. CAMT has strived to serve the society and is determined to bring a revolutionary change in the lives of the poor and underprivileged through various projects and programs.

Testimonials

I am Rita(name changed) residing in the hill station of Nilgiris, Tamilnadu, India. I used to live with my mother, husband and three in-laws. Now that I have lost my mother, my husband is a drunkard, unstable daily wage labourer. This makes my life miserable even to have two square meals a day for the family of 5. I have heard about Martha Tailoring Institute(MTI) a unit of CAMT and completed 6 months diploma training. Now I have the confidence to face the world with my newly acquired skill. I am able to make a decent living through sewing and run the family smoothly with the free sewing machine donated by CAMT.

Rita, Nilgiris
