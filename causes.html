Our Projects

Free Tailoring Classes

This is a six months free training program specially designed for women of ages 16 to 60. No formal education is mandatory. In this program: designing, cutting, pratical and theoretical trainings are offered for 6 months. Forenoon and afternoon class shifts are conducted. This is a very successful program which empowers urban and rural unemployed women to generate income for the upliftment of their families. A certificate of completion is granted to all the candidates after the 6 months training. Free sewing machines are also distributed to the needy. This service has continued to benifit the poor and the unemployed for over the past 20 years with thousands of benificiaries in various village, towns and slums.

Free Water(Borewell/Well) Projects

Hundreds of millions of people across India live without access to clean drinking water. Safe drinking water and improved sanitation can give these families a chance for healthier, happier lives. Comforter Action Mission Trust(CAMT) helps making water accessible to the urban and rural population by drilling borewells and open wells in areas that have no access to potable water. This project has made clean and fresh ground water accessible to the poor promoting health.

CAMT has strived to serve the society and is determined to bring a revolutionary change in the lives of the poor and underprivileged through various projects and programs.

Thousands have been benifited through the different projects and services that CAMT offers.

Medical Camps

India remains one of the third world countries that lacks access to adequate medical facilities for the backward economic sections. The plight of the people with simple to serious ailments in the urban and rural areas is ever increasing. In several cases the ailments are not diagnosed or ignored for lack of awareness. Our organization identifies and takes medical teams to areas where medical care is inaccessible and needed. CAMT conducts medical screening camps and treating simple diseases at the locally available medical facilities and refer specialised cases to hospitals in towns and cities for further treatments. These camps are conducted periodically at various places. We strive to bring medical care closer to even the marginalized and deserving to make a healthier India with each patient treated at our camps.

Free Cataract Eye Camps

Comforter Action Mission Trust(CAMT) in coordination with district administration and 'District Blindness Control Scociety', time to time organizes eye screening for the slum and rural areas and distributes over the counter spectacles free of cost. The objective of these camps is to create awareness about eye disorders and to detect cataract, eye ailments amongst others. Those identified with cataract are taken to the government facilities for free cataract surgeries.

AIDS Awareness Programmes

India has the third-largest number of people living with HIV in the world, approximately 2.1 million. Comforter Action Mission Trust(CAMT) has used a holistic rights-based approach to fight HIV, focussing on prevention and treatment. Our recipients are the all sections of the society including school and college students. Thus the battle against HIV reduces social stigma, improves health, and empower families. CAMT has campaigned the fight against HIV and continues to do so. HIV prevention and awareness programmes conducted by CAMT has largely impacted the society.

Village Sanitation/Hygene Camps

Comforter Action Mission Trust organises awareness camps on sanitation, hygiene and cleanliness in urban and rural areas to create an awareness about self and environmental hygiene. Joining the Indian government's initiative, CAMT continues to make the cleanliness drive of significance. Many needy families are also provided necessary sanitary items to needy families whereever possible. Keeping homes and surroundings neat and clean guarantees healthy growth of children and the general public. Awareness about proper disposal of garbage and the ill effects, the diseases that spread due to the improper disposal of wastes are made.

Community Development

The overall goal of the project is to develop the capacities of the communities to change their own environments, starting among the children and adults, through programs of adult education, nutrition and health, hygiene and sanitation, medical camps. The project includes building and renting community halls where children, teenagers and adults attend various programs and activities. Comforter Action Mission Trust initiates, mobilize resources and executes community project in rural and urban areas for the development of people in a socially responsble manner.

Family Counciling

The family is the most important unit of society. A stable family environment is absolutely essential for the nurture of children. It is here that an individual imbibes values and learns life skills. Comforter Action Mission Trust(CAMT) addresses the issues of domestic violence, marital maladjustment, dowry harassment, domestic disputes, marital problems, depression, stress, suicidal tendencis, etc., by giving free counselling to the affected. Free legal advice,whereever needed, is referred to the experts. The services provided by CAMT helps the community members to seek professional help for their problems.

Employment Generation

Comforter Action Mission strives to imrpove the quality of life for many people in urban and rural areas. CAMT conducts periodical workshops for women and train them in toy making, quilt making, handbag making and also give direct job work contracts from the garment industries to help them generate income and thereby support their families. The Tailoring Training program courses offered by us compliments this effort.

Feeding Programs

There are around 795 million hungry people in the world today. Every year, 3.1 million children under the age of five die due to poor nutrition globally, and 66 million primary school going children attend classes while hungry in developing nations including India. Comforter Action Mission Trust works to prevent malnutrition among underprivileged population in India. Feeding programs conducted by the organization directly benifits the poor and marginalized in villages and slums. CAMT focuses on removing hunger by moving towards sustainable solutions for this malady through our other programs.
