Our Projects

Free Tailoring Classes

This is a six months free training program specially designed for women of ages 16 to 60. No formal education is mandatory. In this programme designing, cutting, pratical sewing and theoretical trainings are offered for 6 months. Forenoon and afternoon class shifts are conducted.

Employment Generation

Comforter Action Mission strives to imrpove the quality of life for many people in urban and rural areas. CAMT conducts periodical workshops for women and train them in toy making, quilt making, handbag making and also give direct job work contracts from the garment industries.

Free Placement Services

The many educated and uneducated, skilled and unskilled work force, generally available especially among the youths are coordinated with local firms, industries, hotels, etc., and placements are secured for the unemployed free of cost.

Free Water(Borewell/Well) Projects in villages and slums

Comforter Action Mission Trust(CAMT) makes water accessible to the remote, rural and slum population by drilling borewells and open wells in areas that have no access to potable water.

Medical Camps

CAMT organises periodical free medical camps in remote villages, towns, slums and schools doing medical screening and treating simple diseases at the locally available medical facilities and refer specialised cases to hospitals in towns and cities for further treatments.

Free Eye Screening and Spectacle Distribution

Comforter Action Mission Trust(CAMT) in coordination with district administration and 'District Blindness Control Scociety', time to time organizes eye screening for the slum and rural areas and distributes over the counter spectacles free of cost.

Free Cataract Surgery Camps

The primary purpose of the outreach is to identify patients’ of curable eye conditions. The program focuses mainly on making eye care accessible to poor patients living in slums and villages.The patients with cataract are operated at the nearest government hospitals free of cost.

AIDS Awareness Programmes

Comforter Action Mission Trust(CAMT) conducts regular AIDS - HIV awareness camps at various places across India. Our objective is a nation free of HIV viruses. We take the message to a cross-section of the society living in slums, towns, villages and also reach out to schools and colleges.

Village Sanitation/Hygiene Camps

Comforter Action Mission Trust organises awareness camps on sanitation, hygiene and cleanliness and also participates through its volunteers in cleaning the environment to join the efforts of the government in "The Clean India" drive across India.

Community Development

The objective of the project is to develop the capacities of the communities to change their own environments for a healthier and financially sound families in the community. Through this we reach out to the children and adults through programs of adult education, nutrition and health, hygiene and sanitation, medical camps, income generating workshops, etc.

Family Counselling

Comforter Action Mission Trust(CAMT) addresses the issues of domestic violence, marital maladjustments, dowry harassment, domestic disputes, marital problems, depression, stress, suicidal tendencies, etc., by giving free counselling to the affected.

Feeding Programs

Comforter Action Mission Trust organizes free feeding programs at regular intervals in needy places to prevent malnutrition among underprivileged population in India. These Feeding programs benifit the poor and marginalized.

CAMT serves the poor and the underprivileged in the country changing hundreds and thousands of families through its over 20 years of relentless efforts.
